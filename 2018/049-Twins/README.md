# 049-Twins

[Twins](http://www.novelgames.com/en/twins/)

* Make pairs with equal sum. E.g. 20
* The Manhattan path must make at most two turns.
* Manhattan paths make 90 degrees turns.

```
a b . .
. . . . 
. d e .
. f g .
```

* ab = 0 turns (ok)
* ad = 1 turn  (ok)
* ae = 2 turns (ok)
* ag = 3 turns (not ok)
